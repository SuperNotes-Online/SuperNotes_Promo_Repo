A beautifully crafted, privacy-first note-taking app. 100% local, zero cloud, zero sign-ups — everything runs right in your browser.

✦ 100% Local-First ✦ Always Free No Account Required Works Offline Open Source
SuperNotes bridges the gap between a simple notepad and a complex knowledge graph. It runs completely offline, requiring no installation, no sign-ups, and no account. With local-first storage, integrated infinite whiteboards, bidirectional wiki-linking, an advanced WYSIWYG editor, a local AI engine, and procedurally generated digital companions — SuperNotes is the ultimate second brain for developers and power users.

Features at a glance

Seven powerful capabilities. All offline. All yours.

01
🔐

Privacy-First & 100% Local

All your notes, checklists, and media live in your browser's persistent IndexedDB storage. You are completely anonymous from your very first keystroke.

  • Zero cloud — no servers, no sync
  • Full offline operation anywhere
  • No emails, no tracking cookies
02
✏️

Integrated Excalidraw Canvas

Launch an infinite vector drawing whiteboard directly inside your text notes with a single click. No context switching.

  • Architecture diagrams & mind maps
  • User flows & wireframes
  • Side-by-side with your notes
03
🔗

Bidirectional Wiki Linking

Type [[ to instantly link any two notes. Hover for live previews. Backlinks tracked in real-time.

  • Simple [[note-name]] syntax
  • Hover previews of linked notes
  • Automatic backlink tracking
04
🤖

Dual Intelligence AI Engine

Two AI modes — a fully private local engine and an optional chat assistant — working together to surface insights from your notes.

  • Local TF-IDF cosine similarity — no API keys
  • DeepSeek chat integration
  • Summarise, brainstorm, refactor code
05
🎭

Mascot Studio & Companions

Procedurally generated digital developer companions with a unique 7-digit serial number and over 1.1 quadrillion possible combinations.

  • Rare DNA serials — yours forever
  • OKLCH neon pixel aesthetics
  • Paste-to-share on X or LinkedIn
06
📧

Multi-Platform Social Export

One-click formatting for X, LinkedIn, Slack, Discord, or Email. Also exports fully styled, self-contained offline HTML files.

  • Platform-aware formatting
  • Self-contained HTML export
  • Looks perfect on any device
07
🌐

Scribens Spellcheck Integration

Click the abc button to launch the world-class Scribens spelling and grammar engine in a new tab for instant, deep proofreading — because your words deserve to be right.

Tech Stack

Everything you need, right in your browser.

🗄️
Storage

IndexedDB

All notes, media, and settings persist entirely in the browser's built-in IndexedDB. Zero server calls, zero leaks.

✏️
Canvas

Excalidraw

Infinite vector whiteboard embedded directly into the editor. Sketch diagrams and mind maps without leaving your notes.

🧠
Local AI

TF-IDF Engine

Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency cosine similarity runs entirely in-browser to surface related notes as you type. No API key required.

💬
Chat AI

DeepSeek API

Optional conversational AI assistant for summarising, brainstorming, and refactoring — connected only when you choose.

🎨
Color

OKLCH

Perceptually uniform color space for rich, consistent palettes across dark and light themes. No washed-out greys.

⚡
Runtime

Vanilla JS + HTML5

Zero frameworks, zero build steps, zero dependencies. Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — runs anywhere a browser runs.

📤
Publishing

Blotato

The premier social media automation & API platform — to schedule your posts and automate your content.
