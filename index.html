// frequently asked

Common questions.

Is SuperNotes Online completely free?

Yes. SuperNotes Online is 100% free with no plans, no paywalls, and no feature limits. You open it in any browser — nothing to install, nothing to pay for.

Does SuperNotes Online store my notes in the cloud?

No. Every note, image, and checklist is stored locally in your browser's IndexedDB. No data is ever sent to a server. Your notes never leave your physical device.

Do I need to create an account?

No account, email, password, or tracking cookies — ever. Open the file and start writing immediately. You are completely anonymous from the first keystroke.

Does it work offline?

Yes, completely. SuperNotes Online is an app with zero external dependencies. Use it on a plane, in a coffee shop without Wi-Fi, or in any environment without internet access.

What AI features does SuperNotes Online include?

SuperNotes Online runs a local TF-IDF (Term Frequency-Inverse Document Frequency) engine that automatically surfaces related notes as you write. No API key, no internet, no cost. It runs 100% in your browser.

How is SuperNotes Online different from Notion or Evernote?

Notion and Evernote store your data on their servers, require accounts, and have plan limits. SuperNotes Online stores everything locally, requires no account, works fully offline, and is free forever — plus it includes a local AI engine and Excalidraw infinite canvas that competitors charge for.

Does SuperNotes Online include a spellchecker?

Yes! SuperNotes Online features an interactive abc 🌐 button directly in the formatting toolbar that links seamlessly with Scribens — one of the most powerful and reliable free online grammar and spelling proofreaders on the web. Clicking it opens your proofreading companion instantly in a new tab.
